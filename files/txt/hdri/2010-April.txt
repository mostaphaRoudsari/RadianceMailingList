April 2010

The CanonHDRcap is now updated and supported under Canon EDSDK version  
2.8. It has been compiled under Mac OS X version 10.5.8 as an  
universal binary, backwards compatible up to Mac OS X version 10.4.x.

Note that Canon EDSDK version 2.8 is now supported for 10.6.x, and  
CanonHDRcap should be able to work under Snow Leopard. Feedbacks from  
users under 10.6.x are most welcome.
